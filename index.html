Are Americans Falling Out of Love?

By Yuliya V. Kim.
Marriage rates have been nationally decreasing for decades. Some say it is because of changes in family structure, others, the economy. While most states stay in a close range of rates, quickie-wedding capital Nevada and destination-wedding paradise Hawaii have their own stories.
